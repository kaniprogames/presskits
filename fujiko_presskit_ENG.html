Pitch

Violet Wisteria is a 90s style platformer for hardcore retro gaming enthusiasts.

Description

Wisteria Asagiri was just a normal girl in suburban Japan when she was whisked away from her human life to fight for the higher realm against the demon Romstead's invasion. After claiming victory and rising to the throne of the higher realm's capital, she must now do battle with the henchmen of the wizard Maskandes who is attempting to conquer both Earth and the higher realm.

Violet Wisteria adds a new twist to the genre of action platformers with its tri-color attack system. Both your reflexes and mental prowess will be pushed to the limit as you take on a myriad of monsters and obstacles across 8 stages of raw action.

History

Violet Wisteria is highly influenced by Nihon Telenet's games, in particular Fantasm Soldier Valis, in which the story is woven tightly into the fabric of the action while never hindering it. The attack system was influenced by Treasure's games like Silhouette Mirage and Ikaruga, and also Super Magnetic Neo on the Dreamcast. The developer Kanipro is a Japan based indie game studio headed by Chris Stark, previously a retro gaming Youtuber under the name Ultra Healthy Video Game Nerd (generally known as "the Hong Kong 97 guy").

Features

  • Completely original attack system which plays like rock scissors paper but with colors instead of symbols.
  • 8 stages with a huge variety of action gimmicks and intricate boss fights.
  • Multiple difficulty settings, as well as unlockable modes and secrets for those who persevere.
